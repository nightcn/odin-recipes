Easy Tuna Patties

Description

These tuna patties are delicious dipped in ketchup, mustard, or hot sauce.
They also great served as a hot sandwich.
Make these simple , elegant, and budget-friendly tuna patties with simple
ingredients you may already have in your kitchen.

Ingridients

  • Eggs: Two eggs lend moisture and help hold the patties together.
  • Lemon juice: Lemon juice brightens up the flavor.
  • Bread crumbs: Bread crumbs will get you that crunchy texture we all like.
  • Cheese: For the most delicious results, grate your own Parmesan cheese.
  • Tuna: You'll need three cans of tuna for eight patties.
  • Onion: A few tablespoons of diced onion gives the tuna patties a bold flavor.
  • Pepper: Enhance the flavor of the tuna patties with a pinch of black pepper.
  • Oil: Fry the tuna patties in a neutral oil, such as vegetable oil.

Steps:

  1. Beat eggs and lemon juice in a bowl; stir in bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese
    to make a paste. Fold in tuna and onion until well combined; season with black
    pepper.

  2. Use your hands to shape tuna mixture into 8 (1-inch-thick) patties.

  3. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry patties in the hot oil until
    golden brown, about 5 minutes per side.
